About Us

My name is Andre Suarez, and I have been in the painting industry for 24 years. I spent 20 years working for the Sherwin-Williams Paint Company and started my own business in 2023. I absolutely love meeting new people and listening to what they want or expect from any painting, powerwashing, or remodeling project. I enjoy helping with ideas, colors, and designs to bring my customers' visions to life. Receiving feedback and hearing what my customers have to say about my work pushes me to grow this business and make ALS a household name.

I am married with children, and being able to take care of my family while seeing their pride in having a dedicated father drives me to work even harder!
